MorseCoder([conf])
Parameters:
conf Optional
{Object} Arguments as associative array.
conf.encodingOverrides Optional
{Object} An associative array of the individual character overrides used to encode. The three characters recognized here are the dit ".", the dah "-", and the element gap " ".
conf.gapOverrides Optional
{Object} An associative array of the gap overrides. The two gaps used are "letter" and "word".
